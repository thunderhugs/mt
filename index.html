About Meowtown

Professional Care With a Personal Touch

Meowtown Cattery is a family-run Cambridge cattery, fully licenced to high welfare standards (Licence No: 25/04678/ANIMAL). We combine high welfare standards with a genuine love for cats, so every guest receives individual attention in a calm, secure environment.

Our heated chalets, sneeze barriers, and outdoor runs are designed around feline comfort. Whether your cat is shy or sociable, we tailor their stay to keep them happy and healthy.
