Pricing

One simple rate for all stays, with clear pricing for multi-cat households. No seasonal surcharges.

£20

per cat, per day

Standard rate per cat.

Sharing rates

£30 for 2 cats sharing

£40 for 3 cats sharing

£50 for 4 cats sharing

What's included: daily feeding (if your cat is on a specialised diet please provide this, otherwise we can cater for most diets such as Felix, Sheba, Whiskas etc), fresh water, playtime, and health monitoring.

Vaccinations & flea treatment: All cats must be up to date with Feline Flu and Enteritis vaccinations. Proof of vaccination will be required on arrival. Flea treatment must also be up to date; we can administer it if due during their stay (please provide this).

Pricing FAQs

Are there any extra charges?

Complex treatments or specialised requirements can be discussed when you book.

How are days charged?

We charge per day stayed, with a minimum stay of 3 days. Drop-off and collection times are flexible and arranged when you book.
