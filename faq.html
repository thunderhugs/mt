FAQ

Booking & Availability

We recommend booking as early as you can, especially for school holidays, half-terms, and the Christmas period. A few weeks' notice is usually sufficient at quieter times.
Yes, if we have space. Email us with your dates and we'll let you know within 24 hours.
Full refund if cancelled more than 2 weeks before arrival. 50% refund if cancelled between 2 weeks and 48 hours before arrival. No refund if less than 24 hours' notice is given. Full details are in our Terms & Conditions.

Health & Vaccinations

Your cat must be vaccinated against Feline Flu and Feline Enteritis before arrival. Proof of up-to-date vaccination is required on arrival.
We can administer straightforward medication (e.g. tablets) at no extra charge. Please bring medication in its original packaging with clear instructions. For complex treatments, discuss with us when booking.
We monitor every cat daily. If we have concerns, we will contact you and, if necessary, our vet. We have emergency vet contact details and will act in your cat's best interest. Any vet costs are the owner's responsibility.
