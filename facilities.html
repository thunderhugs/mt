Facilities

Our Cambridge Cattery facility is designed around feline welfare: each chalet has its own heated sleeping area, sneeze barrier, and covered and secure exercise run so cats can enjoy fresh air in safety. We use covered runs and ventilation gaps between units so cats stay comfortable and healthy.

We also have CCTV, cat trees, shelves, different bedding options, and enrichment, with treats such as Lick-e-Lix and Dreamies liberally given with clients' consent.

Standards

Facility Standards

Sneeze barriers between chalets help prevent the spread of airborne illness. Ventilation gaps allow fresh air while keeping cats secure. Covered runs protect exercise areas in bad weather. We follow strict hygiene protocols: cleaning, disinfection, and separate equipment per cat where appropriate.
