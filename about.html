About Us

Our Story

Meowtown Cattery launched in February 2026. We're a small, independent, family-run business in Lode, Cambridgeshire — and we love cats.

We are a Cambridge cattery, fully licenced to high welfare standards (Licence No: 25/04678/ANIMAL) and offer cat boarding in a calm, secure environment. Every cat gets individual attention; whether yours is shy or sociable, young or senior, we tailor their stay so they feel safe and content while you're away.

Our Philosophy

What We Stand For

Safety First

Fully licenced to high welfare standards, with vaccination requirements, sneeze barriers, and strict hygiene protocols so every cat stays healthy.

Comfort Always

Heated chalets, outdoor runs, and quiet surroundings so your cat can relax as if they were at home.

Individual Attention

We get to know each guest and adapt feeding, playtime, and care to their personality and needs.

Licensing & Standards

Fully Compliant

We are fully licenced to high welfare standards (Licence No: 25/04678/ANIMAL) and comply with local council licensing. All cats must be vaccinated against Feline Flu and Enteritis; we follow Feline Advisory Bureau standards for welfare and hygiene.
